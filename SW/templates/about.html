PieFlasher is an automated chip flashing system for Raspberry Pis.

It can be used either standalone or with an automated system to bulk flash SPI flash chips.

The hardware designs and software are Licensed under GPL 2 and available on Github at:

https://github.com/SeanMollet/PieFlasher/
